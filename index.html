Welcome to CodeFast

CodeFast is India’s leading coding education platform founded by Nikki Shukla. We help students become industry-ready software engineers by providing high-quality courses in Data Structures, Algorithms, Web Development, and Interview Preparation.

Our vision is to bridge the gap between college education and the tech industry through structured learning, expert mentorship, and hands-on projects.

Meet the Founder

Nikki Shukla is a passionate coding mentor and educator, known for his clarity, structured teaching, and commitment to helping students grow in tech. With years of experience and a vision for impactful learning, he built CodeFast to create a learning ecosystem that empowers every student to crack top tech jobs.
