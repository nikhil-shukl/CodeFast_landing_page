About CodeFast

CodeFast is an innovative learning platform founded by Nikki Shukla with a vision to revolutionize how students learn coding and prepare for careers in technology. We understand the challenges students face when transitioning from academic theory to real-world software development, and that's why CodeFast is built around the principle of "learning by doing."

Our mission is to bridge the gap between college education and industry requirements by providing hands-on courses, mentorship from industry experts, and a clear path toward building practical skills. From data structures and algorithms to full-stack web development, we cover it all with structured content and real-world projects.

What sets CodeFast apart is our personalized guidance and result-driven approach. Learners can progress at their own pace, interact with mentors, and join a thriving community of developers, designers, and problem-solvers. Whether you're preparing for product-based company interviews or just starting your coding journey, CodeFast is your trusted partner.

With our focus on outcome-based learning and consistent support, we have helped hundreds of students transform into confident, job-ready developers. Join CodeFast today, and take the next big step toward your tech career with the right skills, the right guidance, and the right mindset.
